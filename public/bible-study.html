Bible Study Tools

If you're like us, you want to dig deeper into Scripture — but life gets busy. We built this tool to help keep you on track.

Whether you're flying solo or part of a study group, here’s what you can do:

  • Create or join a Bible study group with friends
  • Highlight verses and jot down thoughts
  • Stay on top of your reading goals (no guilt-tripping!)
  • Get daily verses and gentle nudges to stay in the Word
