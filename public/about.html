Who We Are

We're just a small team of folks who love Jesus and build stuff. Our aim is to make apps that help people in their daily walk with God — from reading the Bible to staying on track with prayer or planning church events.

Started in a garage (like all great tech stories!), we've grown into a community of believers who love merging faith with function. We're not perfect, but we try our best to point people to Christ through tech.
